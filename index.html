Grover Zampa Vineyards (Bangalore)

Grover Zampa Vineyards is formed as a result of a merger between Grover Vineyards Ltd (GVL) and Vallee De Vin Pvt Ltd (VDV) The group produces around 100,000 cases. Currently the No.2 wine company in the country, Grover Zampa Vineyards is poised to emerge as a strong contender to the market leader, Sula Vineyards Quote: In August 2005, Steven Spurrier quoted "Grover's La Reserve" an 80% CS and 20 % Syrah Blend as best New World Red in Decanter Magazine.
